Now Playing - Ambient, Math-Rock, etc | August 2016

Category: Music     Tags: Ambient, Music,

I’m a big music fan.
I love ambient music more than anything these days. When artists can combine the ambient sound with other elements I melt inside. This is a list of some fantastic music that I’ve been listening to recently :

Toe (Japanese Math-Rock)

This is a ridiculous live DVD, I’m in love with this band.

Rika (Austrian Indie-Stuff)

No clue how I stumbled upon this band, but I can’t stop listening to this album.

Blithe Field (Electronic/Ambient)

Blithe Field consistently makes the most relaxing and mind-bending music out there. No words to describe some of his tunes.

Michiru Aoyama (Japanese Ambient)

What’s with all the incredible music from Japan? This is a gem of an album.
