Full Shopify Store Setup

Contact for a custom quote

I'll build you a great looking and responsive Shopify store, ready to sell your products online with no hassle at all. I work with any of the official Shopify themes and will tweak the theme to fit your company logo and branding. I'll handle all the complicated code and customizations, and by the end of the process, you leave with a beautiful online store ready for customers to purchase your great products.

A standard store setup includes:

  • Setting up a new Shopify account for you (if needed)
  • Installing your responsive theme (free or premium)
  • Customizing the theme with your logo, colours and fonts
  • Adding your products and inventory into Shopify
  • Adding up to 6 info pages to your store (e.g., About, FAQ, Contact)
  • Setting up your product organizational structure (collections)
  • Navigation menus set up with links to your collections and pages
  • Google Analytics setup for SEO and general site statistics
  • Video tutorials showing you how to add/edit products and manage/update your site
  • Includes 2 hours of minor customization/content changes after project end (eg. Change color to green, indent this paragraph, etc)
