EST8 - Meteor/Blaze/Mongo

With a team of fellow students, we were asked by a client to build an up to date real estate listing site with features like a live chat, showing requests, and agent calendars. As team lead I picked Meteor JS as the foundation for our site, as we needed to get a prototype together in a 3 month period - Meteor seemed to be the perfect fit. Our client was extremely happy with the site, and we continue to work alongside him today to bring the project out of the MVP phase. We are currently re-designing the site and waiting to hear back regarding funding and office space in Waterloo, Canada.

  • Meteor JS
  • Bootstrap
  • Blaze
  • MongoDB

Portfolio - Hexo/EJS/Bulma CSS

Once I graduated college and started freelancing, I figured I needed a nice looking portfolio site to display some of my projects and maybe to post some blog articles as well. Wordpress is the obvious option, but I wanted to learn more about static site generators - I found Hexo and really loved the documentation and community support. I started with some pre-made templates, then just decided to build my own theme from scratch using Bulma CSS library. I love the simplicity of this site, and it's extremely quick to load as well.

  • Hexo JS
  • EJS Templating
  • Bulma CSS
