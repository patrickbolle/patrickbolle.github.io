Shopify Stores

The Duke Brothers Coffee

The Duke Brothers, a local company, approached me looking for a responsive design to compliment their vintage style logo and business approach. I am still working with the Duke Brothers to enhance their site and prepare it for the launch of their online sales. You can find them biking around Kitchener/Waterloo selling some of their fantastic cold brew coffee!

Dissolution Information Services

Built for a law service in San Fransisco, California - the client was looking to start selling his service (Marital Property Reports) online and needed a professional looking Shopify site built to convert his customers to the online service.
I built a custom ordering form using Shopify's line item properties, and the client has already converted all of his customers to the site - making upwards of $1000+ a day on Shopify.

Protings.com

Originally built by HippoBrain Design, I was asked to step in to this project to make some major adjustments to the homepage and various other pages on the site.
Working alongside the founder of Protings, over the course of 2 weeks we were able to bring his new vision for the site to life. Fully responsive and focused on the (great) products, Ryan was extremely happy with my work.

Cannahacker Online Store

Cannahacker, a fast growing startup based in the legal cannabis industry in Colorado, came to me looking to expand their business into selling related apparel online. Working alongside their design team, I was able to quickly create a great looking, functional store for them to direct their customers to.
With a large presence in their industry, the Cannahacker team was very happy with my work and we continue to work together today.
